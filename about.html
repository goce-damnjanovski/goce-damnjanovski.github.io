About Me

I am Goce Damnjanovski. I am a student at the Faculty of Computer Sciences and Engineering-FINKI in Skopje. I have previously completed the Master studies in Constitutional Law at the Faculty of Law "Iustinianus Primus" Skopje. Since 2017, I have been learning the skills of Full Stack Web Development on the online educational platform FreeCodeCamp.

I have many years of experience in the NGO sector. I am the founder and vice president of the Association MOTUS and a member of the Executive Board of the School Sports Club Krste Misirkov Kumanovo(USK KMK). I am currently a freelance software tester in "testIO". Earlier in 2020, I worked as a remote data consultant at FLOW Insight in Oslo, Norway.
